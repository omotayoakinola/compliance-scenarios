# GlobalSync Inc. — Compliance Crisis Case Study

## Overview
Acting as newly appointed Chief Compliance Officer (CCO) at GlobalSync Inc., 
a fast-growing multinational technology company operating across the United 
States, European Union, and Brazil. Following a whistleblower tip, I conducted 
a rapid compliance assessment, managed an active regulatory crisis, and 
presented a strategic remediation plan to the Board of Directors.

This case study demonstrates the ability to identify control gaps across 
multiple regulatory frameworks simultaneously, translate legal and technical 
risk into board-level decisions, and develop actionable corrective action 
plans under high-stakes conditions.

---

## Scenario Context
GlobalSync faced four simultaneous compliance failures discovered through 
a whistleblower allegation:

| Allegation | Regulatory Framework | Risk Level |
|------------|----------------------|------------|
| Improper EU-US data transfers | GDPR Chapter V | Critical |
| Bribery of government officials in Brazil | FCPA · Lei 12.846 | High |
| Failure to process data deletion requests | GDPR Art. 17 · CCPA/CPRA | Critical |
| Ineffective financial reporting controls | SOX Section 404 | High |

A major merger with a European company was scheduled in six months, 
making immediate remediation a business-critical priority.

---

## What I Did
- Conducted risk triage across four simultaneous regulatory allegations
- Built a 5x5 risk assessment matrix plotting each allegation by 
likelihood and financial, legal, and reputational impact
- Scoped and structured a full compliance audit across seven business 
units in three jurisdictions
- Designed an evidence-gathering plan including document review, 
interviews, and control testing
- Drafted a formal audit finding using Condition, Criteria, Cause, 
Effect, and Risk Rating structure
- Analysed post-Schrems II data transfer mechanisms and recommended 
the EU-US Data Privacy Framework as the most robust solution
- Assessed CCPA/CPRA private right of action versus GDPR enforcement 
pathways
- Evaluated a Deferred Prosecution Agreement from the DOJ and outlined 
compliance obligations
- Developed a five-item Corrective Action Plan with assigned owners, 
deadlines, and measurable success metrics
- Delivered a board-facing opening statement framing compliance as a 
strategic business imperative
- Recommended Continuous Auditing as the single most important 
future-proofing investment for GlobalSync
