# Compliance Scenarios

Applying compliance frameworks to real organizational problems through 
case studies in audit, data privacy, and security governance.

This repository documents applied GRC work across real-world compliance 
crises, breach incidents, and regulatory frameworks. Each case study moves 
from problem identification to structured audit findings, regulatory analysis, 
and actionable remediation plans, demonstrating the ability to translate 
compliance complexity into decisions organizations can act on.

---

## Case Studies

### 1. GlobalSync Inc. — Compliance Crisis
A multinational technology company facing four simultaneous compliance 
failures across GDPR, FCPA, SOX, and CCPA/CPRA, with a major merger 
at stake. Acting as Chief Compliance Officer, I conducted a rapid 
compliance assessment, managed active regulatory investigations, and 
presented a strategic remediation plan to the Board of Directors.

**Frameworks:** GDPR · FCPA · SOX · CCPA/CPRA · ISO 31000  
**Skills:** Risk triage · Audit scoping · Formal audit findings · 
Corrective action planning · Board-level communication

→ [View case study](./globalsync-compliance-crisis/)

### 2. Globacom vs Finastra — Comparative Breach Analysis
A cross-continental comparative analysis of two real-world cybersecurity 
breaches, examining how organizational culture, regulatory maturity, and 
governance structure shape incident response outcomes across Nigeria and 
the United Kingdom.

**Frameworks:** NDPA 2023 · UK GDPR · GLBA · PCI-DSS · FCA SMCR · 
US State Breach Notification Laws  
**Skills:** Multi-jurisdiction regulatory analysis · Control gap 
identification · Detection and disclosure timeline analysis · 
Remediation roadmap development

→ [View case study](./globacom-finastra-breach-analysis/)
